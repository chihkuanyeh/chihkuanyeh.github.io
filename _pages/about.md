I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Machine Learning Department at Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Pradeep Ravikumar. My research interests focus on building interpretable and efficient models with high performance. I'm also interested in transfer learning, unsupervised learning, and statistical machine learning. Prior to joining CMU, I graduated from National Taiwan University, which I worked with Hsuan-Tien Lin, Yu-Chiang Frank Wang, and Ho-Lin Chen in undergraduate. This summer, I did an internship at Tencent AI lab working with Jianshu Chen and Dong Yu.
